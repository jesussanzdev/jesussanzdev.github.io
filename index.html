About

My name is Jesús Sanz and I live in Spain. Currently, I am developing web projects and applications for different companies as well as contributing to open source projects on the internet.

Some of the technologies with which I currently work are: HTML5, SCSS, Javascript, React, Redux, Gatsby, Bootstrap, PHP or C#.

I love to practice sports such as personal defense, ping pong, hiking or swimming. I collaborate with different non-profit associations and manage various projects to make my community a better place. When I still have some time left, I love watching movies, reading or playing video games.
